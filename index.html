How to buy

Buy XoroCoin today in 3 simple steps

01

Download the Trust Wallet application from either the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store. To add XoroCoin, click on “Add Custom Token” and in the “Network” field, select “Binance Smart Chain”. Paste *the smart contract address* for Xorocoin into the “Contract Address field and click on “Done” to add Xorocoin to your wallet.

02

Navigate to the DApps” tab in Trust Wallet and paste this *link* in the search bar. Once the PancakeSwap website loads, click on “Connect” in the top right corner of the screen and Select “Trust Wallet”.

03

Enter the amount of BNB you want to swap for XORO. Review the details of the transaction and click on “Swap” when ready. Confirm the transaction in your Trust Wallet. Congratulations! You are officially a Xoro Member.
